Membership

Membership in Helping Hands of Glenview is open to all individuals, families, businesses and other organizations. Annual dues, charitable contributions and matching funds are gratefully accepted and are tax deductible. An elected board of directors and officers meet monthly, The members and the community is invited each May to our Annual Meeting. New members and volunteers are welcome to join at any time.
